# User Story 88: Investigation Management Actions

**As a** user saving local investigations,  
**I want** to manage them directly from the investigation panel,  
**so that** I can duplicate, update, delete, and organize my saved work without losing context.

## Acceptance Criteria
- A saved investigation can be updated in place.
- A saved investigation can be duplicated into a new local record.
- A saved investigation can be deleted from the local store.
- The investigations panel shows enough metadata to distinguish records quickly.
- Management actions primarily live in the dedicated `Investigations` section rather than the story-level save panel.
