# User Story 94: Story Save-Focused Investigation Panel

**As a** user saving work from a story,  
**I want** the in-story investigations panel to focus on save/update actions,  
**so that** the story canvas does not duplicate the full investigations browser.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The story-level investigations panel remains available for creating or updating the current investigation.
- Saved-investigation browsing and reopening primarily lives in the dedicated Investigations section.
- The story-level panel does not attempt to be the main cross-story investigation browser.
